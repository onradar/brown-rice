Brown Rice: Complete Guide

Brown rice is a whole grain food packed with nutrients. This guide covers everything you need to know about brown rice — nutrition, cooking tips, and health benefits.

What is Brown Rice?

Brown rice is unmilled or partially milled rice, retaining its bran and germ layer. It has a chewy texture and nutty flavor compared to white rice.

Health Benefits

Rich in fiber, manganese, selenium, and B vitamins. Supports digestion and blood sugar control.
